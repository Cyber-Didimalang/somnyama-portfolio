Somnyama
Arts

Tshepo Inganathinkosi Sethosa, known as "Somnyama" is a talented visual artist from South Africa. Living in this dynamic enviroment, Somnyama has explored various neighbourhoods, which has sharpened his awareness of the lack of art in the city. Each area has influenced his artistery, enriching his perspective on life and culture.

This Art Portfolio is for depict emotions one step at a time, exploring the intersection of modernity and expressions, my visual and contemporary art pieces blend vibrant colors, dynamic textures and thought-provoking themes to capture the essence of our ever-evolving world.

Artist Statement

My story as an artist began when i was 4 years. Just like every boy at that age, I would often draw cars. As time went by i took a liking to drawing faces and cartoons because my older brother would always come back home with drawings he had made at school and I'd try and copy them. They always seemed to amaze me. When I was 15 I started taking art seriously and began trying defferent styles and mediums. I find so much joy in being relatable to my audience through my work. This connection drives me to work harder and search deeper within myself to create something true to myself that can connect to my audeince.

Through my curent art practice, the subject matter that intrigues me most is the fear and discomfort I see in people who are not at ease with themselves, with who they are; and their fear of becoming who they truly are.
